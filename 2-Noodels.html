Beef Ramen Noodle Bowl

Ingrediants -

1) 1 pound beef round steak, thinly sliced.

2) 2 tablespoons cornstarch

3) 2 tablespoon sesame oil

4) 2 packages (3 ounces each) beef ramen noodles, broken into pieces

4) 2 ½ cups water

4) ½ cup soy sauce

4) 3 cups frozen broccoli stir-fry vegetable blend

4) ½ teaspoon ground ginger

4) 2 tablespoons thinly sliced green onion

4) ¼ cup thick-style teriyaki sauce

Method -

step-1 ) In a medium bowl, toss sliced beef with cornstarch and 1 ramen noodle flavoring packet.

step-2 ) Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef. Cook, stirring occasionally, until slightly browned, about 5 minutes. Remove beef from pan and set aside.

step-3 ) In same skillet, combine noodles, water, soy sauce, vegetables, ginger, and remaining flavoring packet. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer until noodles and vegetables are tender, 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.

step-4 ) Return beef to pan. Stir in green onion and teriyaki sauce. Cook another 4-5 minutes to heat through and allow sauce to thicken slightly.
