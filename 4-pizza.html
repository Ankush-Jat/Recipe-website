Pizza

Ingrediants -

1) 1 & ½ cup All purpose flour/ Maida

2) 1 tablespoon Active dry yeast*

3) ¼ cup Milk

4) ¼ cup Water

5) ½ tablespoon Sugar - ½ tblsp

6) ¾ teaspoon Salt - ¾ tsp

7) Olive oil - ⅛ cup Extra virgin or melt butter

8) 2 tablespoon Pasta sauce/ Pizza sauce

9) ½ Onion

10) ½ Tomato Chopped, De-seeded

11) ½ Capsicum chopped

12) 2 Baby corn slit length wise

13) 8 Paneer cubes

14) ½ teaspoon Tandoori masala powder

15) ½ teaspoon Garam masala powder

16) Mozzarella cheese grated, As needed

Method -

step-1 ) Gather the ingredients needed.

step-2 ) In a large bowl, add lukewarm milk, water, oil, sugar and yeast. Mix well using a whisk to dissolve the yeast completely.

step-3 ) If you are not confident about the yeast, I would suggest proofing as suggested below the ingredients table.

step-4 ) Add the flour, salt and gather first. Knead to get a smooth dough.

step-5 ) Keep covered with a wet cloth or cling wrap the mouth of bowl. Set aside untill an hour.

step-6 ) After an hour it will be rised and doubled in size.

step-7 ) Punch down the dough.

step-8 ) Again knead to make a smooth pliable dough

step-9 ) Roll out to a 14 inch disc using rolling pin. I usually roll in such a way that in the middle I thin out and outer part, thick.

step-10 ) Pat the sides to make the circle perfect as well as forming a rim to hold the melting cheese.

step-11 ) Prick with fork in the base to ensure easy cooking.

step-12 ) Spread the sauce generously covering the base, leaving out the rim and sprinkle some cheese. (Optional to spread cheese at this stage)

step-13 ) Arrange the toppings as desired and add the grated cheese to cover the toppings.

step-14 ) Lastly to make it Indian, I sprinkled the masala powders.

step-15 ) Grease a pizza pizza pan with olive oil or just dust with some flour.

step-16 ) Transfer the rolled pizza base to the baking pan. You can roll/ wrap the base in the rolling pin gently and un-wrap in the baking pan

step-17 ) Preheat the oven for 190 degree Celsius (375 F) and bake for 14 - 15 mins..till the base starts to turn golden and the cheese turns golden brown. The base should move freely which indicates a perfectly baked base.

step-18 ) Transfer to another solid surface and cut out into 6 or 8 pieces.
